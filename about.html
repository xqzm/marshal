bio

My name is Nikolay Zhukov, a cinematographer, founder of Marshal video production and a few other projects that help evolve computer graphics in Belarus. For these seven years I was able to realize numerous project in various countries for different brands and artists as well. Passionate about cinematography, videoproducing, photography, traveling, astronomy, and philosophy. Love to realize projects on any step of it. From the developing the script and searching locations, till the very and editing, color grading, adding some visual effects and so on... Can speak english, russian, italian, turkish, and belorussian languages. For more information please feel free to contact me.
